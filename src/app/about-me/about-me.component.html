About Me

I'm Sarah Grimm, I'm 26 years old and I live in the Fresno, California area. I moved to California in 2016 after living on the East Coast for 23 years. While attending West Hills College Lemoore, I was invited to participate in a year-long developing cohort at Geekwise Academy in Fresno. It ended up being the best decision I ever made. I always had a love for coding and design, but I never knew if I could do it. Fast forward to now, I'm an up-and-coming freelance web developer and graphic designer ready to take on the tech world head on. I'm hoping that one day a tech company will adopt me as one of their own. In the mean time, I'm continuing my education with Geekwise Academy and planning to transfer to a university in order to achieve my Bachelor's Degree. When I'm not coding, I enjoy listening to music, playing video games, watching TV, and playing with my dog.

Development isn't just something I do for a job, It's something I love. If you hire me, you'll find that I add a little bit of something that not all web developers can offer, passion.
